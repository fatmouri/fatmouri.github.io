Car Dealership Application


This is a Car inventory application for a company where the HR department can monitor their car inventories. In this application, the HR department needs to upload multiple employees’ names and their driver’s licence numbers to whom they want to give the ownership of the cars. Also, they must upload the car dealership information where they usually buy their cars from. After that, the HR department can upload the details of the car inventories.
